Welcome!

We are California State University, Fullerton's official gaming and esports club!

We are a community for both casual and competitive players, and this is a safe, fun, and inclusive space for gamers alike to meet like-minded people to game and have fun with.

No matter your level of interest in gaming, this is a fun and safe space to socialize!

We hold meetings every other Friday from 6pm - 9pm PST on our Discord and these meetings create an opportunity for everyone to meet others with similar interests and try out new games. Join our Discord to participate in our meetings and find out more about us!
